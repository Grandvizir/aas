Home

Hello and thank you for taking the time to visit our website.

Our service is all about how we can help you at a time when you, your family, friends or work colleagues are in situations where they need some one they can depend on to take very good care of them.

We provide a specialist mental health and medical assistance service for people who wish to be travel between countries, between hospitals, from home to hospital or wherever.

Our service is in the majority staffed by highly trained nurses from different disciplines, often with dual qualifications.

We are here to support you.

We are not an air ambulance service, however we do work with those services accompanying people Internationally who have fallen ill or who require specialised assistance.

Discretion and Privacy are assured. Should you need our services or wish to find out more - please contact us for a private conversation.

Thank you for taking the time to visit our site.
